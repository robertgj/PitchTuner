Detailed Description

Public interface for a simple fixed point or integer audio sample.

Fixed point arithmetic

This section describes the fixed point arithmetic implemented when saSampleBase is not a floating point data type.

Approximate a real number by \(a=A.2^{2n}+A_{h}.2^{n}+A_{l}\) where \(A\) is an integer and \(A_{h}\) and \(A_{l}\) are \(n\) bit integers and the binary point is at \(2^{2n}\).

Approximate multiplication by:

\[ \begin{align*} a.b = & \left[(A.2^{2n}+A_{h}.2^{n}+A_{l}).(B.2^{2n}+B_{h}.2^{n}+B_{l}) \right].2^{-2n} \\ = & A.B.2^{2n} + \ldots \\ & A.B_{h}.2^{n} + A_{h}.B.2^{n} + \ldots \\ & A.B_{l} + A_{h}.B_{h} + A_{l}.B + \ldots \\ & A_{h}.B_{l}.2^{-n} + A_{l}.B_{h}.2^{-n} + \ldots \\ & A_{l}.B_{l}.2^{-2n} \end{align*} \]

Discard the last term and collect the other terms so that:

\[ \begin{align*} a.b \approx & \left[(A.2^{n}+A_{h}).(B.2^{n}+B_{h})\right] + \left[(A.2^{n}+A_{h}).B_{l}\right].2^{-n} + \left[(B.2^{n}+B_{h}).A_{l}\right].2^{-n} \\ = & \left[(a.2^{-n}).(b.2^{-n})\right] + \left[(a.2^{-n}).B_{l}\right].2^{-n} + \left[(b.2^{-n}).A_{l}\right].2^{-n} \end{align*} \]

Approximate signed division by:

\[ \begin{align*} a/b & \approx a.\left[(\frac{2^{4n}}{b}).2^{-2n}\right] \end{align*} \]

Division requires that the length of the standard signed integral type be greater than \((2^{4n}+1)\) bits.
